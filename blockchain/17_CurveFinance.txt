Curve Finance is a Dapp which you can interact with using Metamask.
Curve Finance is an AMM (Automated Market Maker) which allows traders to trade against a pool of money rather than matching buyers and sellers.
You have already read about Constant Product AMM formula. 

Curve finance started initially as a Stablecoin exchange only. They called themselves Stableswap. i.e you could swap USDT, USDC, TUSD without much slippage

Yield Farming
=============
Very trivial. Just mean investing crypto to earn an interest.

Curve Finance is actually a DAO DEX. So there is no CEO/Board of Directors. You can get Curve Tokens in Curve Finance which are kind of governance tokens just like cTokens in Compound.
